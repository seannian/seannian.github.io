I am a recent graduate of San Jose State University, where I earned a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with Honors. Currently, I am an R&D Software Engineer Intern at the PACCAR Innovation Center, where I develop edge-to-cloud data pipelines for commercial vehicle systems. I am seeking a PhD or thesis-based Master's position in computer science for Fall 2025 admission and am very fortunate to be a recipient of the 2025 National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship.

My research interests are broadly in the intersection of distributed systems, computer networks, and machine learning systems. During my time at San Jose State University, I had the privilege of being advised by Dr. Genya Ishigaki and Dr. Ben Reed. Additionally, I conducted research with Dr. Arthi Padmanabhan at Harvey Mudd College as part of an NSF-funded Research Experience for Undergraduates.
